Baker

And that is what they did in Apollo by creating Baker.
They created a library that abstracts away the orchestration so you can focus on implementing the Interactions.
I had nothing to do with it. Apollo made it already before I joined.
As one of the purposes of Apollo they had to make Mobile Onboarding.
Which is that you can become a new customer via an android phone
No need to go to the office
To create that process they had to connect to lots of services which was a recipe for disaster.
That is why they made Baker.
